HOW TO PLAY
Place your bet on the PASS LINE to begin.

On the first roll (the Come Out roll):
7 or 11 = Instant Winner!
2, 3, or 12 = Instant Loss.

If any other number is rolled — 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, 10 — it becomes the POINT.

Now roll your POINT to win.
Roll a 7 before your POINT and all bets are lost.

Upon a 7-out or making the POINT, the game resets with a new Come Out roll.
