What is Last Chad?

Last Chad is a text-based RPG built on NFTs on the Avalanche C-Chain. Battle the Synthette robot invasion using logic & dice, earn XP, level up onchain, and assign permanent battle stats. Each Chad is a one-of-a-kind pixel art warrior — and quite possibly the last line of defence against mechanical oblivion.

How to Mint

  • Go to the Mint page
  • Connect your wallet (Rabby, Core, MetaMask, or WalletConnect)
  • Select how many Chads to mint
  • Each Chad costs 0.02 AVAX
  • Confirm the transaction in your wallet
  • Your Chad will appear in your wallet and on the Battle page

Battle

Visit the Battle page to view your owned Chads. Select a fighter from your gallery and join quests. The Synthettes won't wait — each battle is a real-time dice showdown that determines whether your Chad survives or thrives.

Win fights, rack up points, and earn XP to level your Chad up permanently on the blockchain. Every level is written onchain. Your Chad's progress is yours forever.

Dice Battle Rules

Each encounter round you roll 5 dice to assemble your team. You get 3 rolls per turn.

  • Roll all 5 dice on your first roll
  • After each roll, you can LOCK any dice you want to keep
  • Locked dice are not re-rolled on your next roll
  • You need to roll a 6, 5, and 4 to assemble your team

6 = Weapons — arms your Chad for battle

5 = Armor — protects your Chad from damage

4 = Comms — connects your Chad to the squad

Once you have all three (6, 5, 4), the remaining 2 dice are added together to determine your Team Members score.

If you fail to roll a 6, 5, and 4 by the end of your 3 rolls, no team is assembled and your score is 0.

The higher your Team Members score, the stronger your squad.

XP & Leveling

Every battle earns your Chad XP (Experience Points). Accumulate enough and your Chad levels up — a permanent transaction written to the Avalanche blockchain. No resets. No take-backs. Your grind is immortal.

  • XP is awarded at the end of each battle
  • Level and XP are stored onchain in your Chad's NFT
  • Your current Level and XP bar are visible on the Battle page
  • Higher levels unlock more stat points to assign

Stat Assignment

When your Chad levels up, you earn stat points to assign across four battle attributes. Choose wisely — these points are committed onchain and shape how your Chad fights. The Synthettes are adapting. Your Chad should too.

  • STR (Strength) — raw power. Hit harder, deal more damage.
  • INT (Intelligence) — tactical mind. Outmanoeuvre the machines.
  • DEX (Dexterity) — speed and precision. Strike before they do.
  • CHA (Charisma) — rally the squad. A good leader survives longer.

Visit the Stats page after leveling up to distribute your points and commit your build to the chain.
