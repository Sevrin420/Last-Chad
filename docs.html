Last Chad

What is Last Chad?

Last Chad is an NFT collection of 6 unique fighters on the Avalanche C-Chain. Each Chad is a one-of-a-kind pixel art warrior ready for battle.

How to Mint

  • Go to the Mint page
  • Connect your wallet (Rabby, Core, MetaMask, or WalletConnect)
  • Select how many Chads to mint (1-6)
  • Each Chad costs 0.02 AVAX
  • Confirm the transaction in your wallet
  • Your Chad will appear in your wallet and on the Battle page

Battle

Visit the Battle page to view your owned Chads. Select a fighter from your gallery and enter the arena.

Battle mode is coming soon. Stay tuned for PvP combat, dice rolls, and leaderboards.

Collection Details

  • Name: Last Chad (CHAD)

  • Price: 0.02 AVAX per mint
  • Chain: Avalanche C-Chain
